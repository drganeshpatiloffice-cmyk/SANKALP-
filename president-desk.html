Message to Stakeholders

Dear Friends, Partners, and Well-wishers,

It is a matter of great privilege to connect with you through this digital platform. Since the official registration of Sankalp Gramin Vikas Sanstha in 2018, our organization has been steadfast in its commitment to bridging the socio-economic gap that exists in our rural heartlands. We understand that the strength of our nation lies in the resilience of its villages, particularly in regions like Rashin and Karjat.

Our approach is not merely philanthropic; it is institutional and scientific. Under the expert guidance of our leadership team, we are integrating Dairy Technology and Agri-business innovations to empower farmers with sustainable livelihood models. We believe that by providing the right tools, education, and vocational training, we can transform rural youth into self-reliant entrepreneurs who contribute to the local economy.

Integrity and transparency are the foundations upon which Sankalp is built. By securing our 12A and 80G certifications, we have ensured that every contribution is accounted for and utilized with complete financial discipline. This accountability allows us to execute high-impact projects in healthcare, education, and community infrastructure with the total trust of our donors and supporters.

As we move forward, our "Sankalp" remains clear: to create an inclusive society where dignity and opportunity are accessible to all, regardless of their geographic location. I invite you to join us as we work towards making our villages the beacons of progress for Maharashtra and for India.

With Deep Gratitude,

Mr. Shriram Barbade

President, Sankalp Gramin Vikas Sanstha
