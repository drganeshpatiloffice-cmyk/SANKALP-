Message from the President

Welcome to Sankalp Gramin Vikas Sanstha. Since our establishment in 2018, our resolution has been to drive holistic rural transformation through structured community welfare and scientific innovation. We recognize that true development is multi-dimensional; it requires a simultaneous focus on quality education, sustainable healthcare, and modern agricultural practices to uplift every segment of society.

In Rashin and across the Ahmednagar district, we are implementing expert-led programs—ranging from dairy technology advancements to vocational training for our youth—to build a self-reliant village ecosystem. Our commitment to professional governance is reflected in our 12A and 80G certifications, which ensure that every contribution is managed with total transparency and directed toward long-term social impact.

I invite you to partner with us in this all-round mission. Whether as a donor, volunteer, or well-wisher, your support is vital as we work to ensure that every rural family can live with dignity, security, and opportunity.
