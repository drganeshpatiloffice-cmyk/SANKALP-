    Message from President

    Dear Friends and Well-wishers,

    At Sankalp Gramin Vikas Sanstha, we believe that the true progress of India lies in the sustainable empowerment of our rural heartlands. Since our registration in 2018, our journey has been defined by a single "Sankalp" (Resolution): to bridge the gap between urban opportunities and rural realities.

    Our approach is built on the pillars of Transparency, Accountability, and Scientific Innovation. By integrating modern dairy technology and AgriTech solutions, we are not just providing temporary relief but are building resilient ecosystems for our farmers and youth in the Karjat-Rashin region.

    With our 12A and 80G certifications, we ensure that every contribution is utilized with the highest degree of integrity to drive measurable social impact. I invite you to join us in this mission of building self-reliant villages and a dignified future for every rural citizen.
