From the Desk of the CEO & Secretary

Welcome to Sankalp Gramin Vikas Sanstha. As both the Chief Executive Officer and Secretary of this institution, my dual responsibility is to provide the strategic scientific vision for our programs while ensuring the highest standards of administrative excellence and transparency in our operations.

My academic foundation as a PhD in Dairy Technology has shaped the way we approach rural development. We believe that for rural transformation to be sustainable, it must be driven by scientific innovation and modern management. In regions like Rashin and Karjat, we are focusing on integrating AgriTech and scientific dairy management to empower our farmers, making them competitive and self-reliant entrepreneurs.

Transparency is our core pillar. Our 12A and 80G certifications are not just legal requirements but symbols of the trust our stakeholders place in us. From educational centers to agricultural workshops, every project we undertake is executed with a focus on data-driven impact and long-term socio-economic growth.

I invite you to explore our work and join us in our "Sankalp" to build a more prosperous and dignified rural Maharashtra. Your collaboration is vital in turning our scientific vision into a grassroots reality.

With Sincere Regards,

Dr. Ganesh Patil
